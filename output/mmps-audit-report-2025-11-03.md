## Executive Summary

**Overall AI Visibility Score: 5.1/10** ⚠️ BELOW AVERAGE

Mavrides, Moyal, Packman & Sadkin, LLP (MMPS) demonstrates **below-average AI visibility** despite holding a strong credential (Eric Sadkin - Super Lawyers 2021-2025, 5 consecutive years). The firm achieves strong citation quality (7.7/10) and excellent company profile coverage (100%), but these strengths are severely undermined by weak trust node coverage (45.7%) and near-zero competitive LLM visibility (33% overall, 0% on evaluative/comparative queries).

### Key Findings:

**Strengths:**
- **Eric Sadkin - Super Lawyers 2021-2025** (5 consecutive years) - most valuable credential
- **Perfect company profile coverage** - Website, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, PitchBook all present (100%)
- **Strong citation quality** - 7.7/10, led by Super Lawyers (9.0/10), FindLaw (8.3/10), LinkedIn (8.5/10)
- **Excellent employee culture** - 5.0 Glassdoor rating with positive workplace reviews
- **Strong brand alignment** - 8.8/10 average across all platforms (strongest dimension)
- **U.S. News Law Firm Profile** - Supplemental tier 1 directory presence

**Critical Gaps:**
- **Zero knowledge graph presence** - No Wikipedia, Wikidata, or Google Knowledge Panel (0/4)
- **Zero elite legal directory presence** - No Chambers USA, Best Lawyers, Legal 500 (3/4 missing prestige credentials)
- **Minimal news coverage** - Only case law mentions, no earned media from The Real Deal, Law360, NYLJ, etc. (1/10)
- **Zero professional association visibility** - Not listed in NYSBA, ABA, REBNY, NYC Bar, or Real Property Law Section directories (0/5)
- **Zero discovery visibility on LLMs** - Not appearing when prospects search for "top real estate law firms" or "best commercial transaction lawyers"

### Business Impact:

**Lost opportunity pipeline:** Prospects using AI to research "top NYC real estate law firms" receive competitor recommendations while MMPS remains invisible. Gemini cited 23+ competitors; Perplexity cited 9 competitors; MMPS appeared in neither list.

**Hidden strength underutilized:** Eric Sadkin's Super Lawyers 2021-2025 recognition IS indexed by Gemini (appeared in brand-specific query) but NOT surfaced by Perplexity. This credential could drive competitive visibility if properly amplified through SEO, PR, and structured data.
